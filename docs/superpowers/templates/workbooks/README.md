# Workbook Team Templates

These templates are the shared operating layer for the Workbook Resource Team.

They exist so the team can produce consistent outputs across research, prioritization, workbook design, QA, and funnel packaging.

## Intended flow

1. `workbook-candidate-brief.md`
2. `workbook-opportunity-scorecard.md`
3. `workbook-spec-template.md`
4. `workbook-qa-checklist.md`
5. `workbook-funnel-brief.md`

## Template Roles

### workbook-candidate-brief.md

Used early in the process to capture a workbook idea, target user, problem, competitor references, and why the idea is worth evaluating.

### workbook-opportunity-scorecard.md

Used by the prioritization layer to compare workbook ideas and decide what moves forward.

### workbook-spec-template.md

Used by the workbook architect to turn a chosen workbook into a structured product spec.

### workbook-qa-checklist.md

Used by workbook QA to review the workbook before release.

### workbook-funnel-brief.md

Used by the funnel strategist to package the workbook as a lead magnet and app-entry point.

## Notes

- Keep these templates generic enough to reuse across many workbook types.
- Keep them specific enough that two different agents would produce similarly structured outputs.
- These are shared templates, not final deliverables.
