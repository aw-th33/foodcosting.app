## Our belief

> If you don’t know your food cost, you don’t know your profit.
>

Food cost is the most misunderstood and most important number in restaurants.

We exist to make it **clear, practical, and actionable**.

---

## Our mission

Help restaurant owners **understand**, **calculate**, and **control** food cost — simply and honestly.

---

## What we stand for

- Clarity over complexity
- Calculation over guesswork
- Costing before pricing
- Tools that prove value before asking for money

---

## What we refuse to do

- Chase trends
- Dilute the problem
- Build features without education
- Write content that doesn’t lead to action

---

## Our north star

Everything we publish must help a restaurant operator:

1. Understand food cost
2. Calculate food cost
3. See the consequences of ignoring food cost

If it doesn’t do one of these, it doesn’t ship.
